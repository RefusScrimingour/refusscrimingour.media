The poor victim reported to the local police that he had been raided overnight. “The experience was far beyond horrific, it was heart-wrenching, I can no longer proceed to live my daily life like before!” Peterson whined, recounted the misfortune, as he recovered from the local hospital. “It looked completely inhuman, with pale gooey skin, gigantic AFL sized eyes followed with its razor-sharp clenched teeth and claws.”

The first person to discover David after this atrociously appalling affair was his neighbour, Helena Angelcan. “Oh dear, god knows what happened to poor David last night! His body was dumped viciously in his own garden, with white bubbles foamed all over his mouth, and brutal erythematous scratches all across his body. Peterson offered a short film taken by his security camera; the piece of evidence further testified his description targeting this life shocking event.

This valuable piece of evidence was soon delivered to the Victoria’s Astronomy Association for further investigation. It is still a mystery as to why David was one of the first humans to be victimised by aliens, but their next landings can be anywhere, possibly in your backyard! The reason for their approach to our planet earth is still an unsolved enigma. But one thing is for sure, they are plotting something that will affect not only us but the entire human race…

Are the aliens a sign that our good days are over? Are we just going to sit back and wait while our home is vulnerable to invasion at any second?
